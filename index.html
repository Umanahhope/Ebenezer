About Us

THE VISION

Bringing the love of Christ to the less privileged through service of humanity.

Ebenezer Foundation is a non governmental organization that is geared towards spreading the love of Christ through the act of service. we strongly believe in showcasing and being the expression of God's heart. As the core of our foundation is the children and youths.

The future of these young ones is dependent of their present situation and circumstances and that is why we at the Ebenezer Love Support Foundation have taken up the responsibility to invest in the present that we may create the future made up of not only what we want but also what they desire.
